What is this?

CivicEngager is a tool for looking up who your elected representatives are across the United States. Based on the address, we can find all the federal, state, county and local officials who represent you in government.

We ask you to use this app and offer feedback.

Reporting outdated or missing information

Sometimes data is outdated or missing. If you notice an issue with the data, please report it to Google.
